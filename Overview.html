Downloading

If you click on the PDF/HTML titles above, you can read them online in your browser. The second and third titles have some embedded hyperlinks that link to their prequel(s) or to audio files located on the same server, which you can also view or listen to online in your browser.

If an online PDF/HTML or audio file is open in your browser, you can save it to your local computer. However, if you open the saved copy on your local computer, the embedded hyperlinks will try to find prequel PDF/HTMLs or audio files locally. Therefore it is recommened to download the entire trilogy as a zip package by clicking here. After unzipping, the dowloaded trilogy will be self-contained within a folder.
